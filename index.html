Superior Chemical Resistance

HDPE pipes resist a wide range of chemicals, acids, and alkalis. Unlike metal pipes, they won't corrode, rust, or scale, ensuring pure water quality and extended service life in aggressive environments.

Exceptional Flexibility & Durability

Engineered to absorb impacts and handle ground movements without cracking. Their flexibility makes them ideal for earthquake-prone regions and uneven terrains.

Leak-Proof Fusion Welding

Our pipes are joined through thermal fusion, creating homogeneous joints that are stronger than the pipe itself, guaranteeing zero leaks throughout the system's lifespan.

Advanced HDPE Pipe Manufacturing Process

Our state-of-the-art extrusion technology ensures consistent quality, optimal material properties, and dimensional accuracy in every pipe we manufacture.

High-Grade Raw Material Selection

Vacuum sizing tanks ensure precise outer diameter while internal pressure maintains perfect roundness and wall thickness uniformity.

  • PE100 grade material
  • Optimal molecular weight distribution

Precision Extrusion Phase

Our advanced extruders melt the PE100 granules perfectly, applying exact temperatures to prevent degradation while ensuring a smooth flow.

  • Computerized Temperature Control

Controlled Cooling Baths

The extruded pipe passes through multiple water cooling tanks to solidify the structure without inducing internal material stress.

Vacuum Sizing Calibration

Advanced vacuum chambers pull the molten pipe against sizing sleeves to guarantee exact outer dimensions and perfect circularity.

Inline Quality Control

Ultrasonic sensors continuously measure the wall thickness and diameter 360 degrees around the pipe in real-time.

Automated Marking & Tracking

Laser and inkjet printers apply permanent identification, tracking codes, and standard specification markings directly onto the pipe.

Precision Cutting

Planetary saws provide clean, perfectly perpendicular cuts for exact length requirements without generating excessive dust.

Coiling & Secure Packaging

Smaller diameter pipes are efficiently coiled, while larger lengths are securely bundled for safe transport and field deployment.
